Director of Quality Engineering

About the Role:
Join us as Director of Quality Engineering to transform how we build and deliver quality software. You'll lead a global QE team of 20+ engineers and establish quality standards, automation frameworks, and testing strategies that enable rapid, reliable releases.

Responsibilities:
- Build and lead high-performing quality engineering organization
- Define quality strategy, standards, and metrics across engineering
- Implement comprehensive test automation frameworks and CI/CD integration
- Drive shift-left testing and quality ownership culture
- Establish performance, security, and accessibility testing practices
- Mentor QE engineers and promote career development
- Partner with product and engineering on quality roadmaps

Required Skills:
- 8+ years in quality engineering, test automation, or SDET roles
- 4+ years managing and scaling QE teams
- Expert in test automation frameworks (Selenium, Cypress, Playwright)
- Strong programming skills in Python, Java, or JavaScript
- Experience with API testing and performance testing tools
- Deep knowledge of CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices
- Track record implementing shift-left and continuous testing
- Excellent metrics-driven decision making and reporting

Preferred Skills:
- Experience with mobile test automation (Appium, Detox)
- Knowledge of AI/ML testing approaches
- Background in security testing and SAST/DAST tools
- Experience with test data management solutions
- Contract testing experience (Pact, Spring Cloud Contract)
