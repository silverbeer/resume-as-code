Senior Director of Site Reliability Engineering

About the Role:
We're seeking an experienced SRE leader to drive reliability, scalability, and operational excellence across our cloud-native infrastructure. You'll lead a team of 30+ SRE engineers supporting mission-critical services serving 10M+ daily active users.

Responsibilities:
- Lead and scale SRE organization across multiple product teams
- Define and implement SLO/SLI frameworks and reliability standards
- Drive incident management, blameless postmortems, and continuous improvement
- Architect highly available, fault-tolerant infrastructure on AWS/GCP
- Implement comprehensive observability and monitoring solutions
- Champion automation, infrastructure as code, and chaos engineering
- Partner with engineering leadership on capacity planning and architecture

Required Skills:
- 10+ years of experience in SRE, infrastructure, or platform engineering
- 5+ years leading and scaling engineering teams
- Expert knowledge of Kubernetes, Docker, and container orchestration
- Deep experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Strong background in observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, DataDog)
- Proficiency with Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, Pulumi, CloudFormation)
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines and GitOps workflows
- Strong understanding of distributed systems and microservices
- Excellent incident management and on-call leadership experience

Preferred Skills:
- Experience with service mesh technologies (Istio, Linkerd)
- Background in chaos engineering and reliability testing
- Knowledge of FinOps and cloud cost optimization
- Contribution to open-source SRE tools
- Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA) or similar
